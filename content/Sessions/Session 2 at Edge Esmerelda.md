## orientation

1. Create an account at polycentricity3.endogon.com
2. Confirm your email
3. Click on Games to find the Esmerelda game, and click Join/Enter Game
5. Create an actor
6. Choose a role - Just enter game to go with the random selection.
7. Enter Game

You should now be in the game with role.

## In-game

We'll start the game at 3pm at Boardroom 6 at the Hub.

To start playing, everyone go around and do a quick introduction of your character, it's goals, values. Try to use your own words if you can. 

Once in-game:

Large nodes are Players. The small nodes are Agreements

Menu on the left:
- View your role card
- View all agreemeent details once made

To make an agreement:
Find aligned partners, perhaps with similar goals or values, and start making agreements.

Create New Agreement button in upper right on desktop, or center top if on mobile.

Add Players associated with the agreement, a short description of the purpose of the agreement. For each player, add one obligation they are exchanging, and one benefit they'll receive from the agreement.
