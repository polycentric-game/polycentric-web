# Polycentric

Polycentric is a roleplaying game engine for exploring collaborative governance. Players take on unique roles and negotiate agreements by exchanging obligations and benefits, creating emergent network graphs of cooperation.

The system is adaptable to any context—customize scenarios, roles, and agreement formats to fit your community's unique needs. Transform governance challenges into engaging gameplay.
